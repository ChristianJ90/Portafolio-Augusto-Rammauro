About me

I’m a football analyst, with a special interest in data visualization and the use of advanced metrics for an in-depth analysis of the game, both at a collective and individual level.

I develop attractive, interactive and automated dashboards and reports that anyone can handle without the need for previous knowledge. Everything is customizable and can display information from multiple sources, including eventing, tracking, wellness questionnaires, injury history or any other available data source.

I create performance indexes that summarize a wide range of data in a number from 1 to 10. The stats weighting can be adapted to the customer's needs. The performance indexes can be general, divided into categories (attack, defense, etc.) or adjusted by player position/role.

I provide personalized advice to clubs, federations and agencies who want to develop or improve the use of data for analysis and decision making.
